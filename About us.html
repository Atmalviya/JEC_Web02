About Jabalpur Engineering College

Formely Govt. Engineering College, Jabalpur | Established 1947

Who we are?

We, here at JEC, feel very fortunate to be a part of the institute and have an excellent track record of giving the best engineers to the world, currently working in top organizations. JEC, formerly known as Government Engineering College (GEC) was established on 7th July 1947, is the second Engineering College of the nation, and the oldest engineering institute in Central India. JEC outgrew when the entire global chessboard was witnessing a dramatic change. It was born just a few months before India brokethe shackles of British slavery.
This illustrious Institution got the distinction of being the Second Engineering College in India and is the premier institution of Engineering Education in Central India.
On 7th July 2021, JEC completed this glorious journey of 74 years of producing young engineering talents.
Our college offers various Engineering courses and has a vast skilled student base, making our college prouder every year. We are also giving students from various social and economic backgrounds an opportunity to be part of excellence in higher education.
